Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dried basil, oregano, and parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the baking dish.
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles until al dente.
  3. In a pan, cook the ground beef or sausage with garlic and onion. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the meat and simmer.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, Parmesan, and herbs.
  6. Assemble the lasagna: layer of sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and repeat.
  7. Cover with foil and bake, then uncover and bake until bubbly and golden.
  8. Let it rest before serving.
