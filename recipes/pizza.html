Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce (tomato-based)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni slices (or your choice of toppings)
  • Italian seasoning or basil
  • Olive oil (for crust)
  • Cornmeal or flour (for dusting)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest temperature.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet dusted with cornmeal.
  4. Spread pizza sauce over the dough, leaving the edges for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add pepperoni slices or other toppings of your choice.
  7. Drizzle the crust edge with olive oil.
  8. Bake in the oven until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
